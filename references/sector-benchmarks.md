## Notes on Benchmark Usage

1. **Cyclicality**: These are long-run averages. Cyclical sectors (energy, materials, financials) fluctuate significantly with macro conditions.
2. **Stage**: Early-stage companies often fall outside these ranges (especially growth and margin metrics).
3. **Geography**: US benchmarks. Adjust for international markets (different capital costs, regulatory environments).
4. **Trend direction**: A company improving toward median from below is often more interesting than one at median but declining.
